## What This Means: Beyond the Paradox

**For Charlotte:** CBD protects her neurons because they're under excitotoxic stress (from seizures), but they're not fundamentally broken. CBD stabilizes them, helps them handle the storm.

**For glioblastoma patients:** CBD targets cancer cells because their mitochondria are already failing. It's not killing healthy cells (much) because healthy mitochondria can handle the modulation.

**For drug development:** This opens up a whole new way of thinking about therapeutic selectivity. The targeting happens automatically based on cellular dysfunction.
